Our Story

Built on Passion.
Backed by 13 Years.

Bella Aurora Landscaping & Maintenance was founded in 2012 with one goal — bring creative, professional landscaping to the Rogue Valley that clients can rely on for the long haul. Fully licensed in Oregon with a BuildZoom score of 92, we've built our reputation project by project.

From Medford to White City and the surrounding area, we manage everything — design, installation, maintenance, and digital marketing — so you get one trusted team, start to finish.
